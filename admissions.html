Admissions

Thank you for choosing Nypunya School of Excellence.

Choosing the right school with a right environment for you child is probably one of the biggest and most important decisions as a parent we all make. We, at Nypunya School of Excellence strive with the same goal in mind and we aim to make the transition as easy as possible, and are here to help. Admission to Nypunya School of Excellence is currently open to all children from Pre Kindergarten to Class 9.

Procedure to Register and Admitting your ward
  • Please note that students are considered for admissions according to their age criterion.
  • Collect the Admission Form at the front desk of the school.
  • Fill out the application by affixing proper documents, as specified below :
    • Latest passport sized photograph – 3 No’s. (Coloured)
    • Birth Certificate
    • Past 2 Report Cards (As Applicable)
    • Transfer Certificate of Previous School
  • Submit the Application Form at the front desk of the school.
We would like to inform every parent that we have only limited seats available [Each class would have an average of 25-30 students] from Pre Kindergarten to Class 9. As per the NEP policy and directive of ICSE (The Indian Certificate of Secondary Education).
Exerting any influence for admission, directly or indirectly, will be a disqualification.
We acknowledge the interest of all parents who wish to be a part of NSE fraternity.
To have an effective teaching learning process, we have maintained limited class strength. Considering the limited number of seats available, it is expected that those who seek admission will realize and understand the constraints that the institution faces.
If you are relocating to Mysore and would like to discuss your specific requirements, please do not hesitate to contact the Front Desk, who will be happy to help you.

In case of any queries or clarifications, kindly email the school at enquirynseschool@gmail.com

Written Test Details

Students who appear for the written test will be tested based on logical, analytical, application and general knowledge questions, which are age appropriate, of the subject concerned.

For the selected students, there will be an interview with the Chairman / Principal.

The originals have to be submitted for verification during the interview.

To have an effective teaching learning process, we maintain limited class strength. Considering the limited number of seats available, it is expected that those who seek admission will realize and understand the constraints the institution faces. The disappointment of not getting admission should not become a matter of unhappiness among parents.

Admissions to Classes 1 to 9 are finalized based on the performance in the written test conducted. Normally some classes will have no vacancies at all.

Exerting any influence for admission directly or indirectly will be a disqualification.
